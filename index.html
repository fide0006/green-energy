Solar Energy Technologies

A major solution to increase green energy is the increase in use of solar energy. The sun is the most powerful energy source in the world, solar technologies convert sunlight into electrical energy either through PV panels or through mirrors that concentrate solar radiation. The sun is free and does not require people to pay for it, which makes it the cheapest and affordable sorce of energy.
